# [The landscape of Collective Awareness in multi-robot systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.09321)

## Write a very high-quality and detailed summary of the paper that describes the paper in a way that a human can fully understand. The summary should cover the problem, the proposed solution and highlight the main contributions of the paper.

 Here is a detailed summary of the key points from the paper:

Problem Statement:
- There is a lack of collective awareness in multi-robot systems (MRS), limiting their robustness and resilience to changes in operating conditions. 
- The concept of "collective awareness" is not well-defined in existing literature. There is a need to better understand what constitutes collective awareness and how it can be achieved.

Proposed Solution:
- The authors conduct a literature review to analyze existing research on collective awareness in MRS. 
- They propose a definition of collective awareness that involves individual agent awareness, inter-agent communications, and knowledge integration at the system level for performance improvement.

Key Contributions:
- Identify patterns and gaps in current research on collective awareness in MRS
- Review approaches for collaboration and awareness in different types of MRS (centralized, decentralized, swarm intelligence)
- Summarize the types of knowledge shared between agents (low-level data, intermediate processed data, semantic data)
- Propose an explicit definition of collective awareness in MRS based on individual awareness, communications, and integrated system-level knowledge.
- Highlight open challenges like generalizability across use cases, incorporating uncertainty, ensuring communications, etc.

In summary, the paper reviews the state-of-the-art, proposes a clear definition to standardize future work, categorizes existing approaches, and outlines open problems in applying collective awareness to enhance multi-robot system capabilities.

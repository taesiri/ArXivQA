## Write a very high-quality paragraph summarizing the paper that effectively covers the paper.

 Here is a high-quality paragraph summarizing the key points of the paper:

This paper proposes a novel end-to-end transformer-based architecture called HOI Transformer for human-object interaction (HOI) detection. The key idea is to eliminate the need for separate object detection and interaction classification stages typically used in prior work. Instead, the method directly predicts HOI instances (consisting of human, object, and interaction triplets) in parallel through a transformer encoder-decoder model. A quintuple matching loss supervises the holistic HOI prediction process. Experiments demonstrate state-of-the-art performance on HICO-DET and V-COCO benchmarks, outperforming previous methods without requiring additional pose/language features. Qualitative visualizations reveal the model attends to discriminative regions and handles long-range interactions. By reformulating HOI detection as a set prediction task solvable by a transformer, this work simplifies existing pipelines while achieving strong performance. The end-to-end approach also confers advantages such as out-of-distribution generalization not enjoyed by conventional decoupled detection+classification paradigms.

4) The method achieves state-of-the-art results on challenging HOI detection benchmarks HICO-DET and V-COCO, outperforming previous approaches.

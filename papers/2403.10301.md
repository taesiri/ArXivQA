## Write a very high-quality and detailed summary of the paper that describes the paper in a way that a human can fully understand. The summary should cover the problem, the proposed solution and highlight the main contributions of the paper.

 Here is a detailed summary of the key contents of this paper:

This paper introduces and evaluates Uni-SMART, a novel large language model incorporated with multi-modal reasoning, designed to exhibit superior comprehension abilities for scientific literature. 

The paper first introduces Uni-SMART's model architecture which integrates text, images, and knowledge graphs to enable more accurate context understanding and complex reasoning. It also describes the multi-task pre-training strategy employed to train Uni-SMART using scientific data across diverse domains.  

The main contribution of the paper is a comprehensive evaluation of Uni-SMART across six scientific domains - general chemistry, materials science, biomedicine, drug discovery, and more. A diverse set of tasks are designed to test the model's capabilities in areas like information extraction, reading comprehension, summarization, and question answering based on scientific papers.

The results demonstrate Uni-SMART's ability to understand papers and extract information better than text-only models like GPT-4 and Claude. For example, in materials science, Uni-SMART extracted polymer properties and alloy compositions more accurately. In biomedicine, it showed strengths in tasks like entity recognition, relation extraction, and answering sophisticated biology questions.

Overall, through extensive testing on real-world scientific literature, the paper makes a strong case for Uni-SMART's advanced comprehension abilities compared to its predecessors. The multimodal architecture provides noticeable gains, proving effective in capturing scientific knowledge across disciplines. The model shows promise in aiding future scientific research through its language-image-knowledge fusion strategy.

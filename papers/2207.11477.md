## Summarize the main method used in the paper in one paragraph.

Here is a one paragraph summary of the main method used in the paper:The paper proposes a network slicing model to allocate radio resources across virtual radio access networks (RANs) of multiple virtual network operators (VNOs). The model utilizes a virtual radio resource management (VRRM) module that aggregates and virtualizes radio resource units from different radio access technologies. The VRRM module allocates data rates to the services of each VNO based on a proportional fairness optimization framework, with the goal of maximizing utilization of the total network capacity provided by a common radio resource management (CRRM) module. The optimization uses pre-defined weights for each VNO and service to determine priority in rate allocation, subject to constraints on minimum/maximum data rates per service and overall capacity per VNO based on service level agreement (SLA) contracts. The paper implements this model in MATLAB using the CVX optimization toolbox, and evaluates the impact of varying radio link capacity and number of users on rate allocation to services of different VNOs.

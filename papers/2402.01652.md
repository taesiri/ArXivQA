# [User-Centric AI Analytics for Chronic Health Conditions Management](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.01652)

## Write a very high-quality and detailed summary of the paper that describes the paper in a way that a human can fully understand. The summary should cover the problem, the proposed solution and highlight the main contributions of the paper.

 Here is a detailed summary of the key points from the paper:

Problem:
- Chronic health conditions like diabetes, cancer, etc. require active management plans to control their effects and maintain quality of life. 
- There is large variation in how these conditions manifest and impact individuals based on personal and environmental factors. 
- Existing technology solutions lack personalization and ability to understand user behavior patterns.

Proposed Solution: 
- Adopt a user-centric approach to develop personalized AI models that can capture relevant data from users and environment to understand individual circumstances.
- Combine multi-modal data like wearable sensor data, self-reports, eye-gazing, EEG, etc. based on condition.
- Test approach in constrained settings like education systems and smart cities.

Key Contributions:
- Provided examples of personalized AI to manage conditions like stress, dyslexia in education systems.
- Proposed framework to fuse multi-modal sensory data to enable personalized services.  
- Identified need for better evaluation metrics in ML models that account for individual differences vs just accuracy.
- Presented vision for integrated framework combining sensors in wearables to intelligently monitor chronic conditions.
- Highlighted open problems like practical implementation of explainable & responsible AI.

In summary, the paper argues for a user-centric approach to AI in chronic disease management that collects relevant personalized data to construct customized models per individual for improved treatment.

## Write a very high-quality and detailed summary of the paper that describes the paper in a way that a human can fully understand. The summary should cover the problem, the proposed solution and highlight the main contributions of the paper.

 Here is a detailed summary of the paper "SpectralNeRF: Physically Based Spectral Rendering with Neural Radiance Field":

Problem:
- Classical spectral rendering methods can generate photo-realistic images by modeling light transport, but are limited to rendering only the current viewpoint. Neural radiance fields (NeRF) can synthesize novel views of a scene, but may lose details for complex textures.

- There is a need for a method that combines the benefits of spectral rendering and NeRF to enable view synthesis while preserving details.

Method:
- The paper proposes SpectralNeRF, an end-to-end architecture for spectral rendering using NeRF. 

- It modifies traditional spectral rendering into two steps: (1) Generate a series of spectrum maps spanning wavelengths. (2) Combine the maps for RGB output.

- An MLP network (SpectralMLP) maps an input 5D coordinate to volume density and spectral radiance to construct a spectral radiance field. Volume rendering generates discrete spectrum maps.

- A Spectrum Attention UNet (SAUNet) fuses the spectrum maps into an RGB image to approach the integral calculation in spectral rendering.

Contributions:
- Applies NeRF to spectral rendering in a physically-based way to mutually enhance both techniques.

- Decomposes scenes into spectral components to improve NeRF performance for complex textures.

- Designs a SAUNet using attention mechanisms to effectively combine spectrum maps.

- Provides a spectral dataset of rendered scenes with spectrum maps and RGB images for training and evaluation.

- Achieves state-of-the-art quantitative and qualitative results compared to recent NeRF methods on synthetic and real data.

In summary, the key idea is to represent scenes using a spectral radiance field and neural networks to enable high-quality view synthesis while preserving details even for complex textures. The integration of spectral rendering and NeRF is done in an end-to-end differentiable manner for joint optimization.

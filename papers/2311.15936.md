# [Towards Responsible Governance of Biological Design Tools](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.15936)

## Write a very high-quality paragraph summarizing the paper that effectively covers the paper.

 Here is a high-quality paragraph summarizing the key points of the paper:

This paper outlines regulatory challenges and proposes governance measures to address risks from the misuse of biological design tools (BDTs). BDTs like protein folding and sequence prediction models enable rapid biomedical advances but also present dual-use risks, as their unprecedented accuracy and novel design capabilities could be exploited to cause harm. However, proposals to restrict compute access or utilize export controls may be less effective for regulating decentralized, non-commercial BDT development. The authors present 25 measures across 6 categories—responsible development, risk assessment, transparency, access management, cybersecurity, and investing in resilience—that could mitigate BDT misuse risks. They highlight 7 particularly promising measures: evaluations for dangerous capabilities, vulnerability reporting, structured access, know your customer processes, nucleic acid screening, securing lab equipment, and model-sharing infrastructure. Implementing multilayered governance while avoiding impeding innovation will require coordinated action from developers and governments. Overall, this paper provides a useful framework for conceptualizing proportionate and feasible policies to promote the safe and beneficial development of BDTs.
